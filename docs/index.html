Overview

MyaHealth app is a personalized financial advisor for your healthcare needs.With the help of this app,users can keep track of their charges and their related charges.Mya Health personalized algorithm helps the users to understand the benefits of any additional plans that his/her employer made available.The users also can filter the health care providers based on t he procedure availability,least cost etc.

MyaHealth app is powered by analyzing a lot of data informations like claims data,eligibility data,consumer data, electronic health care records etc from various sources.The backend of mya health uses big data analytics engine that uses Amazon Redshift with a set of predicitve machine learning algorithms to predict the future healthcare cost and robust optimization algorithms.

HIPAA

HIPAA (Health Insurance Portability and Accountability Act of 1996) is United States legislation that provides data privacy and security provisions for safeguarding medical information. The law has emerged into greater prominence in recent years with the proliferation of health data breaches caused by cyberattacks and ransomware attacks on health insurers and providers.
